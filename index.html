About AMT

AMT (Automation Manufacturing Technology) is the One-Stop Automation Solution provider for TE business units to improve productivity, delivery and quality. We innovate, design, develop and build automation machines for TE manufacturing sites globally. Our technology branches include Flexible and Precise Assembly, Low Cost Soldering, Precise Joining, Flexible Feeding, Machine Vision Guided Robotic Assembly, Automatic Cable Preparing and Assembly, Flexible Stitching, AI (Artificial Intelligence) Machine Vision, AI Sensor Toolkit, Flexible Mobile Manipulation System and Material Handling Automation etc.

AI Cup

TE Connectivity's annual AI Cup competition offers a platform for students to showcase innovations in artificial intelligence (AI). The competition aims to foster creativity and problem-solving skills among participants, while simultaneously advancing technology in the field of AI. Students from various disciplines contribute projects that demonstrate cutting-edge advancements in AI. The projects submitted by students are categorized year-wise, allowing for easy access to the latest groundbreaking developments in the field.

Robo Cup Competitions

TE Connectivity's annual Robo Cup competition serves as a platform for students to showcase innovations in robotics. The competition is designed to foster creativity and problem-solving skills, while also advancing technology in the realm of robotics. Students participate by submitting projects that showcase their innovative approaches and advancements in robotics.
